Girlie (Jericho) Lee

The garbage-alley dog who found her way into my heart.

Life So Far...

  • January 1st 2019 - Assumed date of birth; Girlie is born in Suzhou, Jiangsu. Shorthly thereafter she loses her tail in unknown circumstances.
  • January 23rd 2019 - A nameless stray, likely having recently learned to walk, makes her way between a commercial street alley and a construction site dump to be picked up by some guy (Ian) outside a real estate rental agency. Girlie climbs into the stranger's backpack because it is warm. When she wakes 15 hours later she is in her new home.
  • January 26th 2019 - Girlie and Ian move into a new apartment. Girlie pees on a training pad for the first time.
  • Febraury 2019 - Girlie begins losing her baby teeth. Her favorite things during this period of life are seaweed sesame snacks and chasing leaves or other things swept up in the wind.
  • March 2019 - Girlie spends her first nights away from Ian. She stays 11 days with a friend as Ian is off to New York for a conference.
  • May 2019 - Girlie goes on her first road trip to XiShan Island on Tai Lake. She (literally) gets her feet wet but remains undecided on swimming as a hobby at this time.
  • June 2019 - Girlie and Ian take several motorscooter trips to the other side of Stone Lake to search out a bigger appartment away from downtown.
  • July 2019 - Girlie and Ian move into their new place at the Flower City by the Stone Lake. During this period, her favorite things include relocating socks, shoes, parts of shoes, and miscellaneous garbage around the apartment.
  • November 2019 - Girlie is able to connect to the native culture of her adopted father as she is visted by friends from Canada. They take her on extra walks while Ian is at work.
  • December 25th 2019 - Girlie has her first Christmas. Early that morning she, unbeknownst to all parties, meets her soon to be adoptive mother. During this period Girlie's favorite things include chasing laser pointers and visitng Shanto's apartment where she is able to spar with her catfriend- the British short-hair named Poopy.
  • January 2020 - For some reason Ian and Amy stop going to work and stay home with Girlie all day. Peak living ensues.
  • April 2020 - For some reason Ian and Amy slowly being spending less time at home.
  • July 2020 - Girlie travels to JiangJiaGang to live in a hotel with Ian. She is proud to finally be allowed to go to work with Ian.
  • December 2020 - Girlie and her family move to a new apartment. The floors are heated to maximize the potenial nap locations.
  • June 2021 - Girlie gets a Grandma. Girlie proceeds to gain two kilograms. During this period her favorite things include camping, fired chicken, and licking the calves of ladies wearing summer dresses as they walk by. Just one lick.
  • September 2021 - Amy and Ian bring a small, loud, ugly, pink creature home. Girlie is pretty sure the family is already big enough and resolves to ignore the thing until they take it back.
  • December 2021 - The ugly thing, now called Timmy, does not leave and gets a bit cuter. Girlie decides to acknowledge its existence.
